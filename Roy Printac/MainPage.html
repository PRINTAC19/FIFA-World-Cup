FIFA World Cup

The FIFA World Cup is an international association football competition between the senior men's national teams. The tournament has been held every four years since the inaugural tournament in 1930, with the exception of 1942 and 1946 due to the Second World War. in every tournament a different country is chosen to host the tournament. The World Cup is the most prestigious association football tournament in the world, as well as the most widely viewed and followed single sporting event in the world. The contest starts with the qualification phase, which takes place over the preceding three years to determine which teams qualify for the tournament phase. Only 32 national teams qualify for the tournament. The trophy has been won by only eight national teams.
